A message from Dean

Welcome to Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, a centre of excellence where we nurture young talents in the different fields of Information Technology. Our major emphasis of imparting training is to encourage curiosity and innovativeness among our students and lay a foundation from where they can acquire quick learning ability and adaptivity with the fast changing world. We welcome applications from students interested to take admission in our B.Tech programs which is currently offered through CSAB based on JEE (main) merit list. At present we are offering two B.Tech programs- in Information Technology and Electronics and Communication Engineering. Apart from these we admit students from JEE (main) for our various 5 year long B.Tech(IT)-M.Tech(IT) dual degree programs and 5 year integrated M.Tech program in Biomedical Engineering. For our various 2 year duration M.Tech (IT) & M.Tech (ECE) programs and 4 year long M.Tech (IT) –Ph.D (IT) dual degree programs admission is offered through CCMT, a centralized admission process for those candidates having valid GATE score. We also offer few direct Ph.D admissions for highly deserving candidates through our open advertisement. Apart from these we also offer MBA(IT) program through our own admission process for those students having valid CAT score. All our programs are flexible and modern. They offer superb research training along with a strong, and up to date curriculum of course work. Our laboratories are equipped with state of the art equipment and software. They provide a very stimulating environment and are kept open round the clock. Our beautiful campus is superbly networked with excellent speed of internet accessibility. I look forward to working with you as you shape your future during the most exciting period of your life and would like to make sure that your stay with us, would be both memorable and rewarding. Prof G C Nandi (https://sites.google.com/site/gcnandi/)

Research Facilities at IIIT-Allahabad

IIT-Allahabad offers the state of the art infrastructure to its students and scholars for research purposes. The Ph. D cell covers under its ambit inviting applications, short listing and giving admission to candidates as research scholars under the departments of Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Management, Cyber Laws and Information Security, Bio-Informatics and Applied Sciences. The Institute has engaged students under a wide range of heads and disciplines within the Information and related technology domain ranging from pure IT fields such as -Soft Computing, artificial intelligence, Graphics animation and multimedia, Data Mining, Wireless and Sensory Networks, Service Oriented Architectures to Electronics and Communication Engineering topics such as VLSI Fiber Optics, Photonics, Robotics, Computer vision and Embedded systems. The Bio-Informatics Department under its academic concern has research interest in areas like Computational Biology, Structural Biology and Drug Designing, Modeling and Simulations of complex biological structures. The Applied Sciences Department conducts research in areas like Nano Sciences, Genomics, Bio-electrics and mechanics, Quantum Information, Quantum Optics, Pure and Applied Physics. The Management, Cyber Law and Information Security division funds its students to join as research scholars for conducting researches in areas that include Finance and Accounting, Operations, E-governance, Strategic and Cross cultural International Management, Project Management and Human Resources. Candidates with exceptional credits such as a high GATE score, CSIR, JRF, ICAR, DST, INSPIRE Fellowship, publications with good impact factor, prior Publications indicating inclination to Research and development, Software development experience in their areas of interest, or students from institutes of National and International repute are not only encouraged to apply but are giving all possible assistance in their research endeavors in terms of criteria relaxations and funds from state and corporate sponsored projects such as the TCS Research Scholar Program, ISJRP, ISEA and foreign projects like the one involving Carnegie Mellon University and Ministry of Information and Communication Technology, Govt. of India just to name a few. The Human Computer Interaction Department headed by Prof. R.C. Tripathi is responsible for maintaining the Speech and Image Processing Lab and the Graphics and Visual Processing Lab. Devices like Image tracking systems, Head Mounted Device (HMD), Hand Gloves, 6D mouse, Motion Trackers, Face Trackers, Image Manipulators and Matrox servers which greatly assist in the ongoing researches like CUDA based volume visualization, CUDA multi-person tracking, GPU based focus and Context Visualization, Emotion recognition using CUDA, Multi-person Imaging and Image remote Sensing based on fusion techniques. The Labs contain 20 computers facilitated with web cams, headphones and trademark databases. 2 IBM Servers and 12 NVIDIA GPU systems are also provided. The Robotics Deptartment under Prof. G.C. Nandi and Dr. Pawan Chakraborty aim at nurturing the student’s minds towards high quality research in different areas of Information Technology and Complex Artificial Intelligence for making machines that resemble human beings and in near future may take their place. Technologies that are in use include- 1) Computer Control Systems such as Neuro-Fuzzy Controller, adaptive controller and control based on non-linear dynamics. 2) Wireless Communication with sensor networks RFID and Bluetooth based Communication. 3)` Temporal pattern mining and knowledge discovery in Humanoid Robot gestures. 4) Machine intelligence for creating intelligent robots is done using Simulated Annealing, Markov processes, Dynamic Bayesian Network, Artificial Neural network, Fuzzy logic, Genetic algorithm and Support Vector Machines. The Microelectronics Department headed by Prof. M Radhakrishnan, widely deals in the areas of VLSI Design, its Process Simulation and Systems Design for research motives. Facilities include state-of-the-art electronic devices like Non-volatile low power FPGAs, Mixed Signal FPGAs and Low Power FPGAs from ACTEL and Silvaco TCAD software modeling tools. The Tanner EDA Software tools help in Analog ICs, mixed signal and MEMS Design. Standard and Non standard Electric Motors from Spartan Electronics and from Vertex Electronics Inc. are also kept and made use of during research. Facilities like Wafer cleaning, Oxidation, Photo Lithography, Thermal Oxidation Furnaces, packaging and Metallization are also available. Application Specific Integrated Circuits (APIC), Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA) and Micro Electro Mechanical Systems (MEMS) are some of the other facilities that are kept in the labs and are exclusively utilized by students and scholars for research purposes.

Achievements In Research

The Institute facilitates high quality research through collaboration with premier National & International research organization of repute such as: Carnegie Mellon University (Pittsburgh), California University (Riverside, USA), State University of New York, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (USA),Gwangju Institute of Science & Technology (Korea), Canberra University (Australia), EPFL Louisiana and ETZ Zurich (Switzerland), Aalborg University (Denmark), Purvanchal University (Nepal), Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (Ghana), University of Michigan (USA), University of Kaiserslautern (Germany), Caledonian College of Engineering (Muscat, Oman), Russian Academy of Sciences (Moscow, Russia), Erasmus Medical University (Rotterdam, Netherlands), University of Rome “Tor Vergata” (Rome, Italy), University of Abertay Dundee (Scotland, U.K) for global exposure. Indo-Russian Center for Biotechnology (IRCB) Under the umbrella of the Indo-Russian Integrated Long Term Program (ILTP) in Science and Technology, several joint R&D projects are being implemented. Russian institutions have been offering technologies for commercialization in India. Similarly, the Indian side has been offering technologies developed in India for validation and commercialization in Russia. Technology transfer activities in engineering materials, electronics, laser science and technology, catalysis, space science and technology has been significant. The First project at the center is the HCRPDB, a Database Retrieval and Mining System for Human Cell Receptors Proteins. HCRPDB generates information for structure based drug design on cell receptors proteins and allows retrieval of protein sequence related information. Organizations like IEEE, ACM, Science Direct and Springer are some of the few academic bodies that acknowledge the work and research attempts of students engaged at various domains of academic pursual throughout the year. Workshops like IHCI in 2009-2010 and two IITM (Intelligent Interactive Technologies and Multimedia) seminars organized in 2010 and 2013 with the later laying focus on the upcoming MATLAB technologies which found technical sponsors in Springer and ACM respectively are great milestones for the institute. A DRDO sponsored workshop on “Innovation in information communication technologies (ICT) for defense applications” was observed on December 27, 2010. A workshop titled “MATLAB and its application in digital image & Signal processing” was held for 2 days 7-8 March 2013. Institute also organizes various National and International level Workshops and Conferences like International Science Conclave, International Conference for Wireless Sensor Network (IWSN), and International Conference for Intelligent System and Management. ;

Chairman Profile

Shri Ravi Kant

Former Vice Chairman, TATA Motors Shri. Ravi Kant, former Vice-Chairman and Managing Director of Tata Motors and head of the company’s commercial-vehicle business, has played the driving role in Transformation of Tata Motors into a highly successful, well-diversified, and globally ambitious automobile giant. Shri. Ravi Kant, has served as Director of Consumer Electronics at Philips India; Director of Marketing at LML Ltd; Vice President of Marketing at Titan Watches Ltd and the President of Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers. He has been Chairman of IIM Rohtak; Tata Advance Materials Ltd.; TAL Manufacturing Solutions Ltd.; Tata Advanced Materials Limited and Non-Executive Vice-Chairman of Tata Motors Limited. He joined Tata Motors in 1999. He has been Non-Executive Independent Director of Vedanta Limited since January 29, 2015 and served as its Non-Executive Director since January 28, 2014 until January 29, 2015. Ranking and Rewards

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad (IIIT-A) was established in 1999, as a center of excellence in Information Technology and allied areas. The institute was conferred the "Deemed University" status by Govt. of India in the year 2000. The Institute has been conceived with the ambitious objectives of developing professional expertise and skilled manpower in Information Technology (IT) and related areas.As an apex nucleating institute in the area of IT, the establishment of IIIT-A, is a major step of Govt. of India towards strengthening the indigenous capability necessary for exploiting profitably and harnessing multi-dimensional facets of IT at all levels, and attaining expertise to enable the country to emerge as a leading player in the global arena The beautiful 100 acre campus, situated at Deoghat, Jhalwa, designed meticulously on the Penrose Geometry pattern, is being further topped by fine landscaping to give an all round soothing effect to create a stimulating environment. The campus is envisaged to be a fully residential one, with all its faculty, staff and students housed in different pockets. All academic and residential areas are connected to the Institute network.
